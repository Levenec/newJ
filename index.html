Backstory

The New Symbol of Freedom

In one of the most secure places on Earth, where communication with the outside world is a rarity and technology is limited to an old phone with no charge, a new symbol of freedom was born. On an old smartphone, hidden from the watchful eyes of the guards, a coin was created that challenged the system. The token is created under the threat of searches, connection disruptions, and the constant escape from reality. Every JailCoin is infused with the spirit of survival, cunning, and ingenuity.

What does JailCoin symbolize?

  • Freedom — in a world where even air is restricted, freedom matters.
  • Struggle — overcoming systemic barriers.
  • Chaos — creating order from chaos.

Who is behind JailCoin?

It’s not just one person. It’s a community of crypto-idealists who believe in freedom, even when it’s confined by walls and bars.

The story begins with how a phone ends up in a cell. From where — it’s unknown. But it’s on this phone, through artificial intelligence technology, that a revolution is born. Each participant in the project is an "invisible prisoner," whose crypto-escape inspires millions.

The Goal of JailCoin

  • To show that even in conditions of total restriction, breakthroughs are possible.

  • To create a coin that will become a symbol of global crypto-freedom.

  • To unite all those who feel "locked" within the system.
